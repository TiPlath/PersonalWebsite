About

Postdoctoral researcher at University of Twente/University of Münster and Scientific Software Developer, holding a PhD on Virtual Prototyping of Wet Granulation Processes. I am an open-source enthusiast publishing all my research open-access and specialising in accelerating multiscale simulations with complex physics using reduced order modelling, machine learning, and a curious mind.

Research

  • Model Order Reduction for the Discrete Particle Method MORe than DEM
  • Virtual Prototyping of Particulate ProcessesViPr
  • Digital Twins for Additive Manufacturing of Concrete SMATTER

Software

  • MercuryDPM Open-source discrete particle simulation for granular materials – Lead developer
  • MercuryPBM Open-source population balance modelling for granular materials – Inventor and lead developer
  • Contributions to pyMOR and oomph-lib
